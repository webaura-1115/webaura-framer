Privacy Policy

This policy explains what we collect, why we collect it, and how it’s protected.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information like Personal Information Name, email address, phone number, business details—typically collected when you fill out our contact form, request a quote, or sign up for updates and Communication Data like Emails, message, and feedback exchanged during projects or inquiries. Our website also gather non-personal data such as IP address, browser type, and behavior through cookies or third party analytics tools to better understand how visitors engage with our site.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support

Deliver creative services based on your project requests

Communicate about project updates, timelines, and revisions

Improve our website performance and user experience

3. How We Share Your Information

We value your trust and do not sell or trade your personal data. However, we may share it with trusted third parties who assist in our business—such as design tools, email providers, or cloud storage platform. These third parties are only given the information they need to perform their role and are bound by confidentiality agreements. In some cases, we may also share your information with freelancers or contractors working under our direction. If required by law or to protect our agency and users, we may disclose data to legal authorities.

4. Data Storage & Security

Protecting your data is a priority at Landix. We employ industry-standard security practices such as encrypted communication, secure servers, and access controls to keep your data safe. Only authorized personnel can access sensitive project information. While we strive for high security, no system is completely immune to breaches. Therefore, we encourage users to also practice caution when sharing confidential.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location and applicable data laws, you have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You can also object to the processing of your data or withdraw consent at any time. If you'd like to exercise any of these rights have questions about how your data is being handled, please email us at [email protected]. We will respond promptly in accordance with legal guidelines.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or privacy standards.

Updates will be posted on this page with the new effective date

Significant changes may be communicated directly to affected users

Continued use of our website or services implies acceptance of any updates

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, feel free to reach out. We're here to help. Contact us at: [email protected]
